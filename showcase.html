How it works
01 · fan-out

Same prompt, two providers

Every field runs on a cheap model and a strong model in parallel — one OpenAI-compatible gateway.

02 · compare

Agree → accept · disagree → judge

Matching fields are auto-accepted. Disagreements go to a strong judge and are flagged for review.

03 · fail over

A provider drops? Keep going

On a 5xx, 429, or dropped connection the call fails over to the other provider instead of crashing.
